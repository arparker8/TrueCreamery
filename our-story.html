OUR STORY

In the heart of the small town of Parker, True Creamery began as a simple mission to bring back the joy of ice cream to those who had to give it up. When the owner’s daughter, Lily, was diagnosed with lactose intolerance, family ice cream nights suddenly lost their magic. Refusing to let her miss out, the family began experimenting in their kitchen, determined to recreate the creamy, nostalgic taste they all loved without the dairy. After months of perfecting their recipe using all-natural, lactose-free ingredients, they opened True Creamery, a cozy shop that now serves the whole community. What started as a labor of love quickly became a town favorite, proving that great ice cream doesn’t need to come with compromises.

OUR HISTORY

2020

True Creamery opens in Parker

True Creamery opens its doors in Parker, offering handcrafted, lactose-free ice cream inspired by the founders’ mission to create treats everyone can enjoy.

2023

Packaged Ice Cream Launch

True Creamery begins packaging their popular flavors, expanding into local grocery stores and making their ice cream more accessible beyond the shop.

2025

Online Store Launch

The company launches a new website with nationwide shipping, allowing customers across the country to order and enjoy True Creamery’s lactose-free ice cream from home.
